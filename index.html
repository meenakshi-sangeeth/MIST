The Rock Hall of Fame

An overview of legends who defined and shaped the sound of rock music

Nirvana

Pioneers of grunge music, Nirvana shook the world with raw emotion and iconic hits like "Smells Like Teen Spirit."

Radiohead

Masters of experimental rock, Radiohead pushed musical boundaries with albums like "OK Computer."

Alice in Chains

Blending grunge with heavy metal, Alice in Chains crafted haunting melodies and dark lyrics.

Pearl Jam

Known for their powerful live performances and hits like "Alive" and "Jeremy," Pearl Jam remains legendary.

Silverchair

Australian rockers who captured the angst of the '90s with their debut album "Frogstomp."

Pink Floyd

Icons of progressive rock, Pink Floyd is known for conceptual albums like "The Dark Side of the Moon."

AC/DC

High-voltage rock and roll legends with hits like "Highway to Hell" and "Thunderstruck."

Metallica

One of the "Big Four" of thrash metal, Metallica created masterpieces like "Master of Puppets."

Guns N' Roses

Rock legends known for electrifying hits such as "Sweet Child o' Mine" and "November Rain."

Black Sabbath

Pioneers of heavy metal, Black Sabbath's music laid the groundwork for countless bands to follow.

Dire Straits

Masters of storytelling through music, Dire Straits gained fame with songs like "Sultans of Swing."

Led Zeppelin

Legends of rock, Led Zeppelin shaped the genre with anthems like "Stairway to Heaven" and "Kashmir."
